Frequently asked questions

If today I buy a new PC, can I put it under UtopiOS?

Yes, you can buy a new PC with the certainty that it will be compatible with UtopiOS . We designed UtopiOS to be compatible with new devices. Some features of UtopiOS may not be available on all devices. Please consult the device manufacturer for more information.

How much does UtopiOS cost?

UtopiOS is free. You can download it now in the "Download" section.

Can I install UtopiOS on a device already having an OS?


Yes, UtopiOS comes with a program allowing install the OS without conflicting with the previous OS.

Is a minimum configuration required?

No, UtopiOS is designed to be installed on any device . During installation, it will automatically optimize performance based on your configuration.

Will the software be compatible with UtopiOS?


Yes, most of the software is already compatible but we continue to work with other software companies to expand our range.

Will a "Professional" section be available?

Yes, we are currently working with our engineers and partners on this version. It will be available in 2018 and distined to the world of the company. It will offer more advanced features and will be accompanied by the new "UtopiSync" technology developed by our engineers. However, this version will pay off.
